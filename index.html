How to Play

The Rules:

  • You will face $$5$$ real-world travel scenarios in Japan.
  • Your goal is to choose the correct Japanese phrase for the situation.
  • **Strict Scoring:** You only earn **$$1 \text{ point} / \text{scenario}$** if you choose the correct phrase on your **first try**.
  • **Practice:** You can hover over any phrase (correct or incorrect) to hear the audio.

Checkpoint (Save State):

Your progress is automatically saved after every correct answer. You can use the **Menu** button during the game to pause or return to your last save point.
